# Agency Client Interview

Structured interview framework for Agency project discovery.

## Interview Phases

### Phase 1: Business Context
- What does your business do?
- Who is your target customer?
- What problem does this project solve?
- What does success look like?

### Phase 2: Brand Identity
- Do you have existing brand guidelines?
- What tone describes your brand? (professional, friendly, bold, minimal)
- Any competitor sites you admire?
- Colors, fonts, or visual elements you prefer?

### Phase 3: Technical Requirements
- Existing tech stack or preferences?
- Required integrations (CMS, analytics, forms)?
- Performance requirements?
- Accessibility requirements?

### Phase 4: Content
- Do you have existing copy or starting from scratch?
- Key messages to communicate?
- Calls to action?
- Media assets available (photos, videos, logos)?
